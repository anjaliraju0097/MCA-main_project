For whom Yu-Ni Bot Right For?

Yu-Ni would be a great fit for people who are interested in learning CBT techniques, those who need a little bit of support managing their anxiety and stress, and people who like to have an in-app guide.

Experiencing Mild Anxiety or Stress

Yu-Ni can help you build skills to self-manage your mild symptoms of anxiety and stress. Much of the advice the bot provides are genuinely helpful tips and the mindfulness exercises it suggests are easy for beginners.
